About Us

Welcome to PawRides — the easiest and most reliable way to get around for Tigers! Whether you’re running errands in town or planning an out-of-town trip, our ride-sharing platform connects students and drivers to make transportation seamless and stress-free. Designed for the DePauw community, we offer a smarter, more affordable way to travel while building connections along the way.
